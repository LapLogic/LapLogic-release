## Highlights

This release introduces powerful new charting capabilities, improved sync reliability, and various bug fixes that enhance the overall stability of LapLogic.

## New Features

### Enhanced Telemetry Charts

Major improvements to the charting experience:

- **Add Overlays to Existing Charts**: Easily add additional channels to charts
![Chart time/distance toggle](images/v0.1.61/add-channels-to-charts.png)

- **Distance/Time Toggle**: Switch between distance-based and time-based X-axis for your telemetry charts, giving you flexibility in how you analyze your data
- **Chart Resolution Control**: Adjust the number of sample points displayed on charts

![Chart time/distance toggle](images/v0.1.61/chart-time-distance.png)




### Dockable Track Map

The track map can now be docked to the right or bottom of the telemetry analysis view, keeping it visible while you analyze your data. Toggle between docked positions or pop it out to a floating window.

![Dockable track map](images/v0.1.61/dockable-map.png)
