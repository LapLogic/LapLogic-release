## Highlights

This release introduces powerful new charting capabilities, improved sync reliability, and various bug fixes that enhance the overall stability of LapLogic.

## New Features

### Enhanced Telemetry Charts

Major improvements to the charting experience:

**Add Overlays to Existing Charts**

![Add channels to charts](images/v0.1.66/add-channels-to-charts.png)
&nbsp;

**Distance/Time Toggle**: Switch between distance-based and time-based X-axis for your telemetry charts

**Chart Resolution Control**: Adjust the number of sample points displayed on charts

![Chart time/distance toggle](images/v0.1.66/chart-time-distance.png)
&nbsp;


### Dockable Track Map

The track map can now be docked to the right or bottom of the telemetry analysis view. Toggle between docked positions or pop it out to a floating window.

![Dockable track map](images/v0.1.66/dockable-map.png)
